Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 24, 2025

1. Introduction

MeetXBuild ("we," "our," or "us") is a platform connecting startup founders and cofounders seeking to collaborate. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Profile Information (you provide):

  • Full name, age, location (city & state).
  • Profile photo and bio.
  • Skills, technical expertise, and interests (e.g., "React," "AI," "SaaS").
  • Occupation, startup experience, and role preference.
  • Visa status or work authorization (optional; you control visibility).

Authentication & Account Data:

  • Email address and Google Sign-In credentials.
  • Account creation date and profile updates.

Communication Data:

  • Direct messages with matched founders.
  • Swipe history (likes, passes, superlikes).

Device & Usage Data:

  • IP address, browser type, device type.
  • Pages visited, time spent, interactions.
  • Cookies and analytics pixels.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Services: To create your profile, show you potential cofounders, facilitate matches and messaging.
  • Improvements: To understand how you use MeetXBuild and optimize the platform.
  • Communication: To send transactional emails (matches, messages, account alerts).
  • Safety & Compliance: To prevent fraud, enforce our Terms, and comply with legal requirements.
  • Marketing (optional): With your consent, to send updates about new features or events.

4. Visa Status & Sensitive Data

If you choose to share your visa status (H-1B, F-1 OPT, etc.) on your profile:

  • It is visible only to other founders on the Platform.
  • We do not use it for discriminatory purposes.
  • You can update or hide this information at any time.
  • We do not share it with third parties (including immigration authorities) unless legally required.

5. Data Storage & Security

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for secure data storage, authentication, and image hosting. We implement:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Encrypted data storage.
  • Role-based access controls.
  • Regular security audits.

However, no online platform is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

6. Who We Share Data With

We do NOT sell your data. We share information only:

  • With other founders: Your profile (name, photo, skills, interests) is visible to matched founders for collaboration.
  • With Firebase / Google: For authentication and hosting (see Google's Privacy Policy).
  • With legal authorities: If required by law or court order.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data (we will remove it within 30 days, except where required by law).
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, email us at hello@meetxbuild.com

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in.
  • Remember your preferences.
  • Analyze Platform usage via Google Analytics.

You can control cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit Platform functionality.

9. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: Deleted within 30 days (backups may retain data longer for compliance).
  • Legal holds: Data retained if required by law or legal proceedings.

10. Children's Privacy

MeetXBuild is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover we've collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of major changes via email. Continued use of MeetXBuild after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us:

  • Email: hello@meetxbuild.com
  • Address: MeetXBuild, USA
